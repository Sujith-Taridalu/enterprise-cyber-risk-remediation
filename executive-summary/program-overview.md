# Program Overview

This repository packages a portfolio-grade enterprise cyber risk and remediation effort using a Nike-themed business environment. The work combines risk identification, business-impact reasoning, control alignment, and POA&M planning into a structure that looks much closer to a junior GRC program artifact than a classroom submission.

The assessment focused on manufacturing, supply chain, third-party exposure, access control, and broader business continuity concerns. A core strength of the work is that it ties technical weaknesses to operational consequences and then ties those consequences to remediation planning.

## Portfolio Value

- demonstrates security thinking beyond hands-on tools
- shows comfort with controls, ownership, and remediation planning
- communicates risk in business language
- bridges cybersecurity, governance, and operational resilience
