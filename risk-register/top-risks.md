# Top Risks

## 1. Supply Chain Disruption

Nike's global manufacturing and distribution model creates exposure to third-party disruption, vendor weakness, and transportation instability. A cyber event affecting upstream operations could delay production and reduce product availability.

## 2. Access Control Weakness

Privilege management and access sprawl can lead to unnecessary exposure across business systems. The source material highlighted particular attention to access control family controls and the risk of privilege creep.

## 3. Critical Manufacturing Exposure

Digitized manufacturing systems improve efficiency but also create dependencies on secure OT, IT, and partner processes. Weakness in one area can affect broader operational continuity.

## 4. Vendor Security Misalignment

Suppliers and external partners can introduce control gaps if their security posture does not match enterprise expectations. This is especially important in global supply environments.

## 5. Compliance and Reporting Risk

Operational risk is amplified when organizations cannot show clear control ownership, remediation tracking, or defensible governance over identified weaknesses.
