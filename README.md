# Enterprise Cyber Risk Remediation

## Overview

This repository presents a portfolio-grade enterprise cyber risk and compliance remediation program built around a Nike-themed risk assessment, POA&M development, and control-driven mitigation planning. It connects technical and operational risks to governance, business continuity, and compliance outcomes.

## Objectives

- identify and prioritize security and operational risks
- analyze supply chain and critical manufacturing dependencies
- map weaknesses to security controls and remediation actions
- demonstrate structured POA&M planning and accountability
- communicate cyber risk in business-relevant language

## Project Highlights

- Built a Nike-focused risk assessment covering supply chain, information systems, manufacturing operations, and compliance exposure
- Analyzed critical manufacturing and transportation dependencies
- Worked on access control-related control families and POA&M items
- Used a formal POA&M workbook structure with owners, milestones, risk ratings, and target dates
- Connected business operations, regulatory obligations, and security control implementation

## Risk Themes

- supply chain disruption
- third-party/vendor exposure
- operational technology and manufacturing resilience
- access control and privilege management
- compliance alignment and remediation tracking

## Skills Demonstrated

`Risk Assessment` `POA&M Development` `Access Control` `Control Mapping` `Remediation Planning` `Vendor Risk` `Critical Infrastructure Thinking` `Security Governance`
