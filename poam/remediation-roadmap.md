# Remediation Roadmap

## Approach

The remediation approach follows a POA&M model that emphasizes ownership, milestones, target dates, and risk prioritization.

## Priority Areas

### Access Control Improvements

- review over-privileged accounts
- reduce unnecessary access
- implement recurring privilege reviews
- improve accountability for role-based access changes

### Vendor and Third-Party Risk

- align supplier expectations with enterprise security requirements
- improve contractual security language
- validate partner control maturity

### Manufacturing and Operational Resilience

- assess dependencies tied to critical manufacturing systems
- map cyber disruption to business interruption scenarios
- strengthen continuity planning for production-impacting incidents

## Success Measures

- clearly assigned ownership
- trackable milestones
- realistic completion targets
- visible reduction in highest-priority control gaps
