## Rules

Do not upload raw assignment screenshots. Do not include UIN, student ID, personal contact details, professor names, course cover pages, IP addresses, scanner IDs, CVE-like placeholders, Nike logos, or full FedRAMP template pages.

The best screenshots are self-created diagrams or cropped sanitized tables that support the Markdown documentation.
