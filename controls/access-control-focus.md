# Access Control Focus

One of the strongest elements in the underlying project material was the focus on access control. This included both the control-family perspective and the practical risks created when privileges are not reviewed carefully.

## Why Access Control Matters

Access control is not only a compliance topic. It directly affects the size of the blast radius when an account is misused, compromised, or granted more access than necessary.

## Practical Risks

- privilege creep over time
- excessive access to sensitive systems
- weak review processes for changing job roles
- poor visibility into who owns remediation

## Portfolio Relevance

Calling out this focus strengthens the repository because it shows attention to one of the most important and widely understood areas of enterprise security governance.
